Pecan Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 - eggs
  • 1cup - brown sugar
  • 1tbsp - flour
  • 1cup - corn syrup
  • 2tbsp - butter
  • 1tsp - vanilla extract
  • 1.5cup - pecans
  • 1 (9in) - unbaked pie shell

Steps

  1. Heat oven - 350deg. Place pie shell in 9in pan
  2. In med bowl, gently beat eggs. Stir in sugar,
    flour, syrup, butter, vanilla.
  3. Fold in pecans.
  4. Pour mixture into pie crust.
  5. Bake 50-60min. Knife inserted into center should
    come out clean.
