Caesar Salad Recipe

Ingredients>

  • 2clove - chopped garlic
  • 1cup - mayo
  • .3cup - grated parmesan cheese
  • .25cup - half and half
  • 2tbsp - lemon juice
  • 1tbsp - dijon mustard
  • 2tsp - worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Mix together mayo, cheese, half-and-half, lemon juice, mustard, and worcestershire sauce.
    Refridgerate 1 hr.
  2. Serve on top of favorite greens.
