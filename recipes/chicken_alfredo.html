Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Ingredients

Chicken and Pasta

  • 1.5lbs - cubed chicken breast
  • 2tbsp - butter
  • .5tsp - oregano
  • .5tsp - basil
  • .5tsp - salt
  • .5tsp - pepper
  • 16oz - cooked penne pasta
  • .25cup - parsley
  • .25cup - shredded parmesan cheese

Sauce

  • 2tbsp - butter
  • 4clove - minced garlic
  • 3tbsp - flour
  • 2cup - milk
  • .5tsp - salt
  • .5tsp - basil
  • .5tsp - oregano
  • .5tsp - pepper
  • .5cup - shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In pan on med-high heat, melt butter, add chicken. Season w/ salt, pepper, oregano, basil.
    Cook 8-10min until 165deg, remove from heat and set aside.
  2. In same pan on med heat, melt butter, add garlic. Cook until garlic softens.
  3. Add half of flour, stir until incorporated. Add rest of flour, stir.
  4. Pour in milk, little by little, stirring well. Sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, basil. Stir until incorporated.
  6. Add parmesan cheese, pour sauce over pasta, add chicken, mix well.
  7. Serve immediately.
